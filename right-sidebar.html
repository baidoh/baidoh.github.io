International Reserves of Bank

Negara Malaysia

13 October 2017

The international reserves of Bank Negara Malaysia amounted to USD101.4 billion (equivalent to RM428.7 billion) as at 13 October 2017. The reserves position is sufficient to finance 7.5 months of retained imports and is 1.1 times the short-term external debt.
